\section{From $l_0$ to $l_1$}

$\norm{\cdot}_p$: (preferably with pictures of unit balls)
\begin{itemize}
    \item $0 < p < 1$: non-convex, NP, bad
    \item $p > 1$: convex, but doesn't solve the problem in general
    \item $p = 1$: convex, solves the problem, good
\end{itemize}
